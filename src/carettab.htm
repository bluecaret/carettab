Additional Clocks

2nd Clock Shows an additional clock below the primary clock.
2nd Clock Timezone Choose a timezone for this clock.
2nd Clock Label Enter a label for this clock (leave blank for none).
3rd Clock Shows an additional clock below the primary clock.
3rd Clock Timezone Choose a timezone for this clock.
3rd Clock Label Enter a label for this clock (leave blank for none).
4th Clock Shows an additional clock below the primary clock.
4th Clock Timezone Choose a timezone for this clock.
4th Clock Label Enter a label for this clock (leave blank for none).

Note that display of the primary clock and additional clocks is based on the time and timezone your computer is set to. The detection of your current timezone is pretty much an educated guess and may not reflect your actual timezone, thus causing the additional clocks time to be off.
